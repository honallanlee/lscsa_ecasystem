ECA Promotion Fair Web App

Introduction

Since 2016

The ECA Promotion Fair system was previously administrated by the school. In 2016, CCB Chairman Kingo Cheung proposed that the system to be taken over by the Student Association to improve efficiency during the fair.

Developer

Allan Lee, Treasurer of Student Association 2016-2017. The Data Management Team of the  Finance Committee was established as part of my policy to digitalise the operations of the Student Association. Contact Information can be found in the footnote if assistance is required.

Data Management Team

The Data Management Team has been the core backbone of the system as it maintains the digital operations within the Student Association. It created various programmes for Talent Quest, Christmas Ball and other internal projects. It is hoped that with the focus on STEM in curriculum that the Data Management Team can grow bigger in the future.
